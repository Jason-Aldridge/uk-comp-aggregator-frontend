Why we exist

UK prize draws are scattered, opaque, and hard to compare.

If you've ever tried to find a worthwhile competition, you know the problem: dozens of operators, hundreds of live draws, no consistent way to know which ones are actually good value. Ticket prices that hide the real odds. Cars listed as £100,000 when the cash alternative is £40,000. Instant-win mechanics buried in tiny print.

RaffleRadar was built to cut through that. We pull data from every major UK competition site, normalise it, and surface the draws that genuinely give you the best shot — by prize value, odds, time remaining, and cash alternatives.

What we do

Three things, done well.

Track everything

Live data from every major UK competition operator, refreshed continuously throughout the day. Prices, ticket counts, prize value, closing times.

Score real value

A single value-ratio score that combines prize value, ticket price, tickets sold, and cash alternatives — so you can compare any two draws at a glance.

Surface the best

Filter by category, closing window, price, free entry, or value score. Find competitions that match what you actually want to win.

How it works

From operator site to your screen, in four steps.

1

We scrape every active competition

Our pipeline runs against eleven UK operators on a rolling schedule — more frequently for competitions closing soon, less for those days away. No manual entry, no stale listings.

2

We normalise the data

Different operators use different formats for prize values, cash alternatives, and ticket pricing. We unify it all into one schema so comparisons are meaningful.

3

We calculate a value score

Every competition gets a value-ratio based on real prize value (not headline price), the cost per ticket, tickets remaining, and time to draw.

4

You decide where to enter

We don't sell tickets and we don't run draws. We surface the data. Every entry happens on the operator's own site — we just help you choose where to go.

What we stand for

Independent. Transparent. Operator-neutral.

Independent

We're not owned by any operator. The data and the rankings are ours alone.

Transparent

Our value-ratio method is documented. If you want to know why a draw is ranked where it is, you can see it.

Operator-neutral

No paid placements. No "featured" slots bought by operators. The best draws float to the top because they're the best draws.

Player-first

We're built for the person buying the ticket, not the company selling it.
